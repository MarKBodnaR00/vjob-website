VJob – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

VJob ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the VJob mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address
  • Profile information: profile photo, CV data, work preferences
  • Videos: introduction videos uploaded by users
  • Location: city/region for job matching purposes
  • Device information: device token for push notifications
  • Usage data: app interactions, job views, likes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the VJob service
  • To match job seekers with employers
  • To send push notifications about matches and messages
  • To verify user identity and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication). Data is stored on Google's servers in accordance with their security standards.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • Between job seekers and employers within the app for matching purposes
  • With Google Firebase as our infrastructure provider

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app settings, which will permanently delete all your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

VJob is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
